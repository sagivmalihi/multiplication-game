## Overview

### English Summary
Princess Arielle's peaceful kingdom of Numeria was protected by 10 magical crystals, each powered by the ancient art of multiplication. One day, the grumpy wizard Balagan (meaning "chaos" in Hebrew), who never understood math and resented its power, scattered the crystals across the kingdom's 10 magical regions. Without the crystals, the kingdom fell into confusion—doors won't open, bridges collapsed into riddles, and magical creatures became restless and blocked all paths.

Arielle discovers she is the only one who can restore the crystals because she possesses the rare gift of "Koach HaKefel" (the Power of Multiplication). She must journey from her small village through 10 enchanted lands, solving multiplication challenges to calm confused creatures, open magical locks, and ultimately reach the Crystal Palace to reclaim her throne.

Along the way, she discovers that Balagan scattered the crystals not out of malice, but because he felt left out—he never learned multiplication and felt ashamed. In the end, Arielle teaches him that it's never too late to learn, and he becomes her trusted Royal Mathematician.

### Hebrew Summary (תקציר)
הנסיכה אריאל חיה בממלכת נומריה השלווה, שהייתה מוגנת על ידי 10 גבישים קסומים הפועלים בכוח הכפל. יום אחד, הקוסם הזועף בלגן, שמעולם לא הבין מתמטיקה והתרעם על כוחה, פיזר את הגבישים ברחבי 10 האזורים הקסומים של הממלכה. אריאל מגלה שרק היא יכולה להחזיר את הגבישים בזכות הכוח הנדיר שלה - "כוח הכפל". היא יוצאת למסע דרך ארצות קסומות, פותרת אתגרי כפל כדי להרגיע יצורים מבולבלים, לפתוח מנעולים קסומים, ולהגיע לארמון הגביש כדי לתבוע את כס המלכות שלה.

## Characters

### Main Protagonist: Princess Arielle (הנסיכה אריאל)

**Hebrew Name**: אריאל / הנסיכה אריאל

**Age**: 7 years old

**Personality Traits**:
- **Brave** (אמיצה) - Never backs down from a challenge
- **Kind** (טוֹבַת לֵב) - Treats everyone with compassion, even confused creatures
- **Curious** (סַקְרָנִית) - Loves learning and discovering new things
- **Determined** (נחושה) - Never gives up, even when problems seem hard
- **Encouraging** (מְעוֹדֶדֶת) - Always believes in herself and others

**Background**: Arielle grew up in a small cottage at the edge of the kingdom, not knowing she was a princess. When the crystals were scattered, her multiplication powers awakened, and she learned the truth about her royal heritage. Now she must prove herself worthy of the crown by restoring order to the kingdom.

**Special Power**: כוח הכפל (Koach HaKefel / The Power of Multiplication) - When Arielle solves multiplication problems, she creates magical energy that can calm creatures, unlock doors, build bridges, and restore the crystals.

**Visual Notes for Designer**:
- Anime-inspired design with K-pop demon hunter aesthetic
- Practical adventure outfit (not a fancy princess dress)
- Magical glow when using multiplication powers
- Different expressions: determined, happy, thinking, victorious

---

### Main Antagonist: Balagan the Wizard (הקוסם בלגן)

**Hebrew Name**: בלגן (means "chaos" or "mess")

**Age**: Very old (appears grumpy elderly wizard)

**Personality Traits**:
- **Grumpy** (זַעְפָן) - Always complaining and frowning
- **Lonely** (בּוֹדֵד) - Isolated himself because of shame
- **Secretly Kind** (טוב בסתר) - Deep down, he's not evil, just hurt
- **Ashamed** (מִתְבַּיֵּשׁ) - Never learned math, feels embarrassed
- **Redeemable** (בַּר תִּקּוּן) - Capable of change and growth

**Background**: Balagan was once a young wizard who tried to learn math but struggled. Other wizards laughed at him. He grew bitter and decided that if he couldn't use multiplication magic, no one should. He scattered the crystals hoping to prove that the kingdom didn't really need math. But watching Arielle's journey, he slowly realizes he was wrong.

**Role in Story**:
- Appears in visions/projections throughout the journey, taunting Arielle
- Never directly harms anyone
- Creates obstacles and awakens confused creatures
- His magic is "confusion magic" - he scrambles and mixes things up
- In the end, accepts Arielle's offer to learn multiplication

**Visual Notes for Designer**:
- Classic wizard appearance with chaotic elements
- Robes with mixed-up patterns (numbers floating around him randomly)
- Expressions: grumpy, surprised, eventually smiling
- Not scary - more comical and sympathetic

### Supporting Character: Pip the Number Sprite (פיפ השדון)

**Hebrew Name**: פיפ

**Role**: Arielle's companion and guide

**Personality**:
- **Cheerful** (עַלִּיז) - Always optimistic and encouraging
- **Helpful** (עוֹזֵר) - Gives hints and celebrates victories
- **Loyal** (נֶאֱמָן) - Never leaves Arielle's side

**Background**: Pip is a small magical sprite made of numbers. He was freed when Arielle's powers first awakened and pledged to help her on her quest. He provides tutorial hints, encouragement when she gets answers wrong, and celebrates enthusiastically when she succeeds.

**Function in Game**:

- Provides encouragement on wrong answers ("!נסי שוב, את יכולה")
- Celebrates on correct answers
- Narrates story transitions

**Visual Notes**:
- Small floating sprite made of glowing numbers
- Changes color based on mood (blue calm, gold excited, soft pink encouraging)
- Simple, cute design

## World Setting

### The Kingdom of Numeria (ממלכת נומריה)

A magical kingdom where mathematics is the source of all magic. Multiplication is the most powerful form of math magic, and the 10 Crystal Keepers (ancient rulers) created the 10 Power Crystals to maintain harmony.

**The 10 Regions** (from Arielle's village to the Crystal Palace):

1. **Emerald Meadows** (אחו האזמרגד) - Rolling green hills near Arielle's home
2. **Whispering Woods** (יער הלחשים) - Enchanted forest with talking trees
3. **Crystal Caves** (מערות הבדולח) - Underground caverns with glowing crystals
4. **Floating Gardens** (הגנים המרחפים) - Islands floating in the sky
5. **Rainbow Falls** (מפלי הקשת) - Waterfalls of every color
6. **Desert of Echoes** (מדבר ההדים) - Sandy dunes where sounds repeat
7. **Frozen Peaks** (פסגות הקרח) - Snowy mountains with ice castles
8. **Twilight Marsh** (ביצת הדמדומים) - Mysterious swampland of purple mists
9. **Starlight Bridge** (גשר הכוכבים) - A bridge through space to the palace
10. **Crystal Palace** (ארמון הגביש) - The royal palace, Arielle's true home

## Story Premise

### The Inciting Incident

*Seven years ago*, Queen Liora gave birth to Princess Arielle during a rare cosmic event—the Alignment of Ten Stars. The baby was blessed with the Power of Multiplication. But Balagan, jealous and bitter, scattered the 10 crystals that very night and cast a fog of confusion over the kingdom. In the chaos, Arielle was hidden away in a small village for her safety.

*Now*, on Arielle's 7th birthday, she solves a multiplication puzzle by accident—and her powers awaken, burning away the fog around her village. Pip appears and reveals her true identity.

### The Quest

Arielle must travel through all 10 regions, finding each crystal. In each region:
1. The crystal has caused local creatures to become confused and block the path
2. Arielle must calm/help the creatures by solving multiplication problems
3. Each solved problem weakens the confusion spell
4. After 10 encounters, the region's crystal is restored
5. The crystal grants her passage to the next region

### The Stakes

- Without the crystals, the kingdom remains in chaos
- Balagan's confusion magic grows stronger each day
- Only Arielle can restore balance with her gift
- The people of Numeria are counting on her

**Multiplication Focus**: Tables 1-3 (easy introduction)

**English Summary**:
Arielle's adventure begins in the gentle green meadows outside her village. On her 7th birthday, she accidentally solves a multiplication puzzle and discovers her magical gift. Pip the Number Sprite appears and explains that she is the lost princess. The meadow's creatures—bunnies, butterflies, and friendly field mice—have become confused by Balagan's spell and are running in circles, blocking the path. Arielle must use her newfound power to calm them and find the first crystal hidden in an ancient oak tree.

**Story Arc**:
- **Opening**: Arielle's 7th birthday, powers awaken

- **Tutorial Encounters**: Gentle creatures, simple multiplication (1-3 tables)
- **Crystal Discovery**: The Emerald Crystal glows in response to her power
- **Closing**: First success! Pip celebrates, they see the forest ahead

- Confused Bunnies (ארנבונים מבולבלים)

## Monster Types

### Design Philosophy
- Never scary or threatening
- Confused/lost rather than evil
- Cute or endearing designs
- Become happy/friendly when "defeated" (calmed/helped)
- Match their environment aesthetically

### Monster HP Scaling
Based on architecture.md difficulty config:
- Chapter 1-3: 5-6 HP (easier, fewer riddles needed)
- Chapter 4-6: 6-7 HP (medium)
- Chapter 7-9: 8-9 HP (harder)
- Chapter 10: 10 HP (final challenge)

### Monster Dialogue Template

Each monster has three dialogue states:

1. **Intro** (before battle):
   - Shows confusion/problem
   - "!אני כל כך מבולבל" (I'm so confused!)
   - "?איפה אני? מה קורה" (Where am I? What's happening?)

2. **Hit** (correct answer):
   - Shows progress toward clarity
   - "!אה... זה מתחיל להיות ברור" (Ah... it's starting to become clear!)
   - "!עוד קצת... אני מרגיש יותר טוב" (A little more... I'm feeling better!)

3. **Defeated** (calmed/helped):
   - Shows gratitude and clarity
   - "!תודה רבה, נסיכה אריאל" (Thank you so much, Princess Arielle!)
   - "!הבילבול נעלם! אני חופשי" (The confusion is gone! I'm free!)

## The Ending

### The Final Confrontation

**Setting**: The throne room of the Crystal Palace

**Balagan's Last Stand**:
When Arielle enters the throne room, Balagan is there, surrounded by chaotic magic. But he looks more sad than angry.

**Dialogue** (Hebrew with English translation):

**Balagan**:
```
"הגעת עד לכאן? אף אחד לא היה אמור להצליח!"
```
(You made it this far? No one was supposed to succeed!)

**Arielle**:
```
"למה עשית את זה? למה פיזרת את הגבישים?"
```
(Why did you do this? Why did you scatter the crystals?)

**Balagan** (reveals his pain):
```
"כי... כי אני מעולם לא הצלחתי בכפל. כולם צחקו עליי. חשבתי שאם אני לא יכול, אף אחד לא צריך לדעת..."
```
(Because... because I never succeeded at multiplication. Everyone laughed at me. I thought if I can't do it, no one should know how...)

**Arielle** (with compassion):
```
"אבל זה לא מאוחר מדי ללמוד! אני יכולה ללמד אותך!"
```
(But it's not too late to learn! I can teach you!)

**Balagan** (surprised):
```
"את... תלמדי אותי? אחרי כל מה שעשיתי?"
```
(You... would teach me? After everything I did?)

**Arielle**:
```
"כל אחד יכול ללמוד. זה מה שגיליתי במסע שלי."
```
(Everyone can learn. That's what I discovered on my journey.)

### The Transformation

Balagan accepts. He tries a simple multiplication problem with Arielle's help. When he gets it right, his chaotic robes transform into orderly patterns. The Crown Crystal floats down and accepts Arielle as queen.

### The Coronation

**Pip narrates**:
```
"ואז קרה דבר נפלא. בלגן הפך מקוסם זועף למתמטיקאי המלכותי. הוא עכשיו עוזר לילדי הממלכה ללמוד כפל, ותמיד מזכיר להם - אף פעם לא מאוחר מדי ללמוד!"
```
(And then something wonderful happened. Balagan transformed from a grumpy wizard into the Royal Mathematician. He now helps the kingdom's children learn multiplication, and always reminds them—it's never too late to learn!)

### The Celebration

All the creatures from every region come to celebrate. The 10 crystals orbit around the palace, shining brightly. Colors, music, and joy fill the kingdom.

**Final Message to Player**:
```
"כל הכבוד, אריאל! הצלחת להשלים את כל ההרפתקה! את אלופת הכפל האמיתית!"
```
(Congratulations, Arielle! You completed the entire adventure! You're the true multiplication champion!)

### Post-Game

After completion, the player can:
- Replay any chapter for practice
- See their statistics (mastery of each multiplication fact)
- Enjoy "free play" mode with random encounters
